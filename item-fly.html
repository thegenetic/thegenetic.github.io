Flappy Bird
fly through

A chilled bird overcomming obstacles

Hey folks, this is a 2d bird game though you must have known about this game its all over the internet.

So I have used pygame for building this game. In this game there is a bird which has to fly through some piles, if it succeds (I control it using space bar) then there is an addition in the points else you are out. It stores the highest score and displays it on the top of the screen. This piles produces randomly in any time interval outside one end of a screen and approaches towards the bird.

Its fun to play with your friends(one after another). Give this a shot. Try building it on your own. Thank You.
